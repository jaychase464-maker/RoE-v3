# Development Roadmap

A milestone closes only after committed implementation, clean Unity compilation, validators, tests, smoke tests, earlier regressions, and clean Console evidence.

## Prototype milestones

- Milestone 0 — Project Foundation — Complete
- Milestone 1 — First-Person and Tactical Interaction — Complete
- Milestone 2 — Weapon and Force-Event Foundation — Complete
- Milestone 3 — Suspect, Civilian, Compliance, and Arrest — Complete
- Milestone 4 — Officer AI, Commands, Door Traversal, and Bounded Initiative — Live prototype passed and committed

### Milestone 5 — Mission, ROE, and After-Action Review — Integration Candidate

Implemented: mission/policy assets, four objectives, evidence aggregation, pure evaluators, reasoned ROE findings, transparent scoring, mission phase, automatic and manual report finalization, an in-world debrief console, diagnostics, setup, validation, build gate, and tests.

Exit pending: Unity compilation; setup/validator; all tests; peaceful and force-review scenarios; Milestones 1–4 regressions; clean Console; commit and push.

### Milestone 6 — First Tactical Vertical Slice

Goal: one short, replayable residence or storefront incident with multiple entry choices, incomplete intelligence, an uncertain armed suspect, a civilian, officer initiative, arrest/force outcomes, and complete after-action review.

Planned foundations:

1. Mission briefing and selectable entry point.
2. Multi-room portal and clearance graph.
3. Objective/event persistence for restart and replay comparison.
4. Production-ready operation start/end flow instead of prototype auto-start.
5. First environment art pass only after the multi-room contract is stable.

## Expansion after the vertical slice

Negotiation; patrol perimeter and traffic; medical/EMS; K9; drones; sniper/spotter; bomb technicians; evidence and detectives; command post; career consequences; multiplayer after authority and determinism are replication-ready.
